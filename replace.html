At International Auto Glass we use only the finest quality glass in every windshield replacement we do. We follow material and installation specifications to return your car to its OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) specifications. At International Auto Glass, we know that using the highest quality materials combined with state-of-the-art installation techniques not only makes you the customer happy, but will keep you and your passengers safe in the event of an accident.

With our convenient location in Lynnwood and free mobile service to the Seattle-Everett area, we can replace a windshield for any year, make, and model (both foreign and domestic). FAST

Windshield repair saves time. A windshield repair takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete. A windshield replacement usually takes approximately one hour on average to complete.

SAFE

Windshield repairs do not alter the integrity of the original factory seal around the windshield since the glass is never removed.

AFFORDABLE

Windshield repair saves money. The price of a repair is a fraction of the cost of a windshield replacement. In fact, most insurance companies will even waive the deductible.

CONVENIENT
