About Us

Family Owned Since 1992

Lynnwood, WA's Auto Glass & Window Tinting Experts!

International Auto Glass is a local family-owned and operated auto glass and window tinting business. We have been servicing the auto glass & window tinting needs of the Long Beach, California area since 1992. Over our many years of business, International Auto Glass & Window Tint has established a reputation as the local experts in auto glass repair, auto glass replacement, and residential window tinting. The local communities have come to know us and trust our service beyond any other auto glass and window tinting company.
